In case you need clarification

Is xFetishHub completely anonymous?
Yes. We prioritize your privacy above all else. xFetishHub operates as a secure sandbox. Your viewing history and generated statistics are stored in a secure, encrypted cloud environment to allow synchronization. When used as a PWA, your session is isolated from your main browser history.
How does the recommendation algorithm work?
Think of it as "Tinder for Adult Content". Our AI analyzes the metadata of the videos you like versus those you dislike. Over time, it builds a vector profile of your tastes to suggest new genres you might enjoy but haven't discovered yet.
Does it work on iPhone and Android?
Absolutely. xFetishHub is optimized for mobile use as a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it directly from Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android) by selecting "Add to Home Screen". This creates an app icon that launches in full-screen mode, separate from your main browser history.
Is the content hosted on your servers?
No. xFetishHub is an intelligent aggregator and analytics tool. We do not host video files. We provide a smart interface to discover content that is publicly available on major adult networks, acting as a sophisticated search engine for your preferences.
What is included in the Free vs Pro plan?
The Free plan includes basic daily generations and privacy features. The Pro plan unlocks unlimited AI generations, detailed "Wrapped" style history reports, advanced filtering, and deeper customization options for the player interface.
